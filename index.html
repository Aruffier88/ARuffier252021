Meadows of Dan, Virginia

A short drive South!

Meadows of Dan, Virginia is a small town on the border of Virgina and North Carolina tucked away in the Mountains. Everything closes early, the street lights shut off at night, and there are no big businesses to be found for quite a drive. If you're looking to really get away and be one with nature, this Hidden Gem is the spot!
